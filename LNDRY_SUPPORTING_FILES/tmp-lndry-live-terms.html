Section 02

Platform & Services

Lndry is a technology platform that connects urban customers with local, verified garment-care businesses ("Partners"). Lndry acts as an intermediary marketplace and facilitates:

  • Scheduling and managing garment care service bookings
  • Real-time order tracking and status updates
  • Secure payment processing
  • Customer support and dispute resolution
  • Quality assurance and Partner vetting

Services are currently available in select Indian cities and are expanding. We do not guarantee service availability in all areas.

Section 05

Garment Care & Liability

Lndry takes garment care seriously. However, the following limitations apply:

  • Pre-existing damage: Lndry is not liable for damage that existed before pickup. Our Partners will note pre-existing conditions at the time of collection.
  • Care Label Compliance: You are responsible for ensuring garments are submitted with accurate care label instructions visible. Lndry Partners follow care labels as provided.
  • Delicate & High-Value Items: For items valued over ₹5,000, please declare the value at the time of booking. Liability for high-value items is limited to the declared value or ₹5,000 whichever is lower, unless premium coverage is purchased.
  • Maximum Liability: Lndry's aggregate liability for any damage claim is capped at 5× the service fee paid for the specific order in question, subject to verification.
  • Claims Process: Damage claims must be raised within 24 hours of delivery with photographic evidence. Claims raised after this window may not be entertained.
  • Prohibited Items: Lndry does not accept items contaminated with hazardous materials, biohazards, or substances that violate Indian law.
Section 06

Cancellations & Refunds

  • Customer Cancellation (before pickup): Orders cancelled more than 2 hours before the scheduled pickup receive a full refund to the original payment method within 5–7 business days.
  • Customer Cancellation (after pickup): Once garments have been picked up, cancellation is not possible. You may still raise a complaint if service quality is unsatisfactory.
  • Lndry Cancellation: If Lndry or a Partner cancels your order, you will receive a full refund or credit, plus a courtesy discount on your next order.
  • Quality Issues: If garments are returned in unsatisfactory condition, raise a complaint within 24 hours. Verified quality issues will result in a re-service at no charge, or a partial/full refund at Lndry's discretion.
  • Subscription Refunds: Subscription fees are non-refundable. However, if you cancel a subscription within 48 hours of first purchase and no services have been used, a full refund may be granted.
