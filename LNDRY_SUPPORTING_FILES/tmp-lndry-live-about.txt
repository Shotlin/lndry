Our Story
We're Reimagining Garment Care for India.
A lean, bold team of builders obsessed with solving a problem
1.4 billion Indians deal with every single week — laundry.
The Beginning
Born from frustration. Built for scale.
India's urban workforce — the fastest-growing in Asia — loses 4–6 hours every week to laundry. The existing infrastructure is fragmented, unreliable, and stuck in 1990.
We asked a simple question: What if getting clean clothes was as seamless as ordering food?
That question became Lndry — India's first AI-powered garment-care platform that connects urban professionals with vetted local laundry partners through a seamless, tech-driven experience.
We're not just a laundry app. We're building the wardrobe-care operating system for modern India — a platform that empowers thousands of local laundry entrepreneurs while serving millions of urban households.

🧭 Direction
Our Mission
To make professional garment care effortless, affordable, and accessible for every urban Indian — powered by technology and delivered through local communities.
We do this by building the technology layer that connects urban customers with trusted local laundry businesses — creating a win-win ecosystem where quality, convenience, and livelihood all go up together.
Our Vision
To be the wardrobe-care operating system for 500 million urban Indians — the invisible infrastructure that keeps India's workforce looking and feeling its best.
Just as the internet became invisible infrastructure for communication, Lndry will become invisible infrastructure for garment care — seamlessly integrated into the fabric of urban Indian life.
