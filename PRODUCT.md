# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Urban Indian customers in Pune who want laundry, dry cleaning, ironing, and garment-care support without calling multiple shops or guessing quality. Secondary users are local laundry vendors who want better digital demand, clearer order flow, and a trustworthy partner acquisition channel.

## Product Purpose

LNDRY is a recommendation-led laundry marketplace. Customers enter their address and service need, then LNDRY presents an eligible nearby partner and a clear booking path. Success means the website feels credible enough for early customers, partners, payment review, and client presentation while staying honest about what the platform currently supports.

## Brand Personality

Premium, calm, operationally sharp.

## Anti-references

Do not make the site feel like a generic AI landing page, a crowded vendor-comparison portal, a coupon-heavy laundry website, or a fake “super app” with unsupported claims. Avoid fake live tracking, exaggerated metrics, repetitive cards, decorative animation, and unverified founder or customer claims.

## Design Principles

- Show the careline: explain the journey from service selection to OTP handover with visual evidence, not vague promises.
- Recommend, do not overwhelm: the marketplace should reduce decision load instead of making customers compare many vendors.
- Trust comes from operations: legal details, support paths, service limits, order status, and partner verification should be visible.
- Premium should feel useful: motion, imagery, and layout must clarify what happens next.
- Indian context first: imagery, area names, prices, service language, and trust cues should feel built for Pune launch users.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA, visible focus states, keyboard-friendly navigation, readable 16px-plus body copy, reduced-motion support, and responsive layouts from small phones to desktop. Do not rely on color alone to communicate state.
